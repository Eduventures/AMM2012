Program Description

The Annual Member Meeting is the central networking and learning component of Eduventures' Learning Collaborative membership.
Join us to:

  • Delve into the latest higher education research
  • Network with peers and Eduventures' staff
  • Discuss key trends and challenges and their potential impact on institutions and higher education overall
