The Learning House, Inc. is the expert in creating, managing, and growing online degrees for colleges and universities.

Partnering with more than 100 colleges and universities, Learning House offers a business model that enables institutions to efficiently and affordably achieve their online education goals.

Learning House provides a comprehensive solution to success, including curriculum development and management, faculty training and professional development, marketing and lead generation, admissions and enrollment management, student retention, 24/7 technical support, learning management systems, and management and consultation.

Instructor-centric Curriculum Development
Since Learning House's founder was a professor, we have always been dedicated to a very collaborative process for developing online curriculum. Choose from a library of professionally developed online courses or custom online courses based on your face-to-face curriculum. Our quality review process and technical inspections contribute to the quality of your online courses.

Faculty Training and Support
Learning House provides a comprehensive professional development program for instructors. The program ensures that instructors are comfortable with the online environment before their courses launch. In addition, instructors can access an exclusive instructor hotline and the online Help Center for 24/7 technical support.

Dedicated Cross Functional Service Team
To ensure the success of your online education program, we dedicate a team of employees to manage and consult on all aspects of your program. As a Learning House partner, your team consists of the following employees: Relationship Manager, Course Designer, Client Services Specialist, Training Specialist and Marketing Coordinator.

Learning House Core Services

Learning House product offerings are built from a selection of our eight core services:

Curriculum Development & Management
In collaboration with administrators, we develop a curriculum strategy and a schedule of courses. Our quality review process and technical inspections and review develop a structure for continual online course improvement.

Faculty Training & Professional Development
We provide on-demand and live learning management system (LMS) and pedagogy training for online instruction, including best practices.

Marketing & Lead Generation
Our performance-driven marketing approach helps develop a strategy for finding quality students for your online programs.

Admissions & Enrollment Management
Our team guides prospective students through every step of the admissions process until they have enrolled in and started their online program.

Student Retention
We design our retention services to improve the overall learning experience at your institution and help students achieve their educational goals.

24/7 Technical Support
We provide 24/7 support to students, faculty members and administrators via live assistance, including phone, chat and email support through our online Help Center.

Learning Management Systems
We create a user-friendly, interactive online learning environment through an enhanced version of Moodle, which we host and support.

Management & Consultation
With more than a decade of experience, we provide tools and resources that contribute to the success of your online education programs.
